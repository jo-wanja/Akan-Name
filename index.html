The Akan Naming Tradition

About

The Akan are a tribe from Ghana.They are known to frequently name their kids based on the day they were born. this is an old tradition that was preserved even as Africans were being sold all over the Carribean.in Jamaica, the old naming system is still used and this is evidenced by the popular Jamaican singer Koffee. Other famous people getting their names from the system include Kwame Nkurumah the first president of Ghana, and Kofi Anaan the former UN Sec-general.

The Male Name-Day Pair

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

The Female Name-Day Pairs

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
